About Us

LL.B. (Bachelor of Laws)

Our LL.B. program at Shree D.D. Kotiyawala Municipal Law College, Porbandar, is a comprehensive three-year undergraduate course that provides students with a solid foundation in legal principles and practices. The program covers a wide range of subjects essential for a career in law, including constitutional law, criminal law, civil law, and more.

LL.M. (Master of Laws) in Human Rights and Duties

Our LL.M. program with a specialization in Human Rights and Duties is designed for law graduates who wish to deepen their understanding of human rights law, international humanitarian law, and related legal frameworks.

Why Choose Us

Join Us

Explore the Bachelor of Laws (LL.B.) and Master of Laws (LL.M.) programs with a specialization in Human Rights and Duties. Whether you are passionate about defending human rights, promoting social justice, or shaping legal policies, our programs provide the knowledge, skills, and opportunities.

Expert Faculty

Our faculty members are experts in human rights law, with extensive academic and practical experience. They guide students through cutting-edge research.

Practical Learning

Students gain practical experience through internships with human rights organizations, participation in moot court competitions.

Global Perspective

Our LL.M. program exposes students to global perspectives on human rights issues, preparing them for careers in international organizations.

Research Opportunities

Students have opportunities to engage in research projects, contribute to our law journal focusing on human rights.

Career Development

Our dedicated placement cell assists students in securing internships and job placements in prestigious organizations.
